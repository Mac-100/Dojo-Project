About

The Dojo is a free learning platform where kids explore the exciting world of computer science and information technology. Designed for ages 8 to 16, our mission is to make tech education accessible, engaging, and empowering — no matter a child’s background or experience level.

We offer interactive, self-paced courses that teach kids how to code, build websites, understand hardware, stay safe online, and think critically about technology. Our content is designed to be fun and beginner-friendly, using games, projects, and real-world challenges to keep learners motivated.

At The Dojo, we believe every child deserves the opportunity to build their digital skills and confidence. With guidance from experienced mentors and support from a growing community, students can discover their potential and spark a lifelong interest in tech — all for free.
